Kimchi Fried Rice

This easy kimchi fried rice recipe is a classic korean lunch! Delicious meal packed with flavor from korean chili paste (gochujang) and proteins involved! Not only does this recipe taste amazing, it's nutritious as well. Packed with protein, carbs, and fibers. Perfect for summer!

Ingredients

  • Sesame Oil
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Pork Belly
  • Gochujang
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • Kimchi
  • Eggs
  • Sesame Oil
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Furikake
  • Cooked Rice

Steps

  1. Place all pork marinade ingredients into mixing bowl, whisk together, and add pork.
  2. Mix together until all pork pieces are fully coated. Let marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Pour vegetable oil into large skillet over high heat. Add pork and saute for 4-5 minutes until it has browned. Remove from skillet ad set aside.
  4. Clean pan and pour vegetable oil into skillet and beaten eggs. Gently stir as eggs cook. Remove from heat and add eggs to cooked pork belly.
  5. Pour sesame oil into skillet. Stir in kimchi and soy sauce and saute until kimchi caramelizes.
  6. Add rice and stir together until rice is coated. Add pork belly and eggs back to skillet and stir together.
  7. Stir in 2 scallions and sesame seeds and you're ready to serve!
